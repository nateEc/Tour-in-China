Chinese Hamburger(hinese Hamburger, Rou Jia Mo (肉夹馍) means “meat in a bun,” which sort of makes it similar to what we'd think of as a hamburger, or maybe a Chinese sloppy joe. So there's the meat filling of shredded pork belly with spices and fresh herbs sandwiched between a homemade pan baked bun.)
